ABOUT

We are a team of data scientists currently attending Lambda School, an intensive online data science course for a career in applied statistics and predictive modeling. Building real, ready-to-ship products with a team of other students generating real work experience is a key feature of Lambda School. This app is a product of that feature.

Gathering historical booking data from AirBnB we have built and trained a predictive model. This model allows a user to find and predict an optimal price of a property based on variables the user inputs such as location, property type, beds, bathrooms, etc. The predicted price can then be used to gauge a recommended listing price for owners of properties, or for an expected value of a property for those looking to stay in a particular city.
